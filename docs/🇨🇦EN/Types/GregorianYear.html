SDGCornerstone GregorianYear
Structure

GregorianYear

A Gregorian year.

Declaration

struct GregorianYear

Discussion

Initialize with a negative integer to get a BC year. The provided mathematical functions automatically accomodate for the lack of a zero year. For example:

let adOne = GregorianYear(1) let oneBC = GregorianYear(−1) let oneYear = Int(1) XCTAssertEqual(adOne − oneYear, oneBC) XCTAssertEqual(adOne − oneBC, oneYear)
